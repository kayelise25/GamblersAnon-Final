IMPORTANT: IF FIRST-TIME USER CLICK HOW TO PLAY BUTTON!!!

Blackjack hands are scored by their point total. The hand with the highest total wins as long as it doesn't exceed 21; a hand with a higher total than 21 is said to bust. Cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value, and face cards (jack, queen, king) are also worth 10. An ace's value is 11 unless this would cause the player to bust, in which case it is worth 1

Home Screen
Before you click the start button you have the option to select which gamemode you would like to play between: regular mode and tutorial mode (which is our newest mode which tells you which decision you should have made after you play which is based off our strategy card)

This is the Betting Screen
Here you set the deck size to any number between 1 and 10. If nothing is entered the default is 4. There are 2 options for betting which is chip or manual input **note: if you use the chips it will not appear in the manual input part**
***IMPORTANT NOTE: DO NOT CLICK BUTTON MULTIPLE TIMES IF MOVING SLOW IT WILL WORK OFF FIRST CLICK***

GamePlay Screen
This is a demo of a hand being played. The top card is the dealers and the bottom two are yours (the player). On the right of that is the decision buttons, if its grayed out it means its disabled.
**NOTE: DO NOT CLICK HIT BUTTON MULTIPLE TIMES IF MOVING SLOW IT WILL WORK OFF FIRST CLICK**

Win/Lose Screen
This screen pops up after you win or lose. It shows how much you won from that hand. To exit just click anywhere on the screen (that's NOT the image).

Restart Game
After you exit win/lose screen a dropdown menu of pokerchips will appear to place a new bet. The X in the top right is how you return back to the home screen

Split Decision
When pressing a split decision your cards split and your decision splits aswell. Play the first hand and stand when done, then play the second hand and stand when done.
